about scott.

I discovered flight simulation in 2020 and immediately connected with an exceptional community of aviation enthusiasts. What began as a new interest has evolved into a lifelong passion.

My primary focus is flying CMA CGM air cargo operations, operating realistic cargo routes that align with my professional interests in international logistics and French commerce. I specialize in simulating state cargo operations, replicating the precision and protocols required for sensitive freight missions.

Current Operations:

  • CMA CGM Air Cargo routes (real-world flight schedules)
  • Boeing 777F (B77L)
  • International cargo operations with French connections
  • State cargo simulation flights

Previous Airlines:

  • American Airlines
  • Qatar Airways
  • Transavia Airlines

The shift to dedicated cargo operations reflects my appreciation for the logistical complexity and international coordination these flights require. Flight simulation offers me a unique way to explore the technical and operational aspects of aviation while connecting with fellow enthusiasts worldwide.

This hobby site is separate from my professional work. All content reflects simulated operations only.
