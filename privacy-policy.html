Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

The App collects:

  • Your Discord User ID (to link your profile)
  • VATSIM pilot data you authorize us to access (callsign, position, aircraft, flight plan)
  • Technical data (timestamps, API request logs)

2. Data Sources

All flight data is retrieved from the publicly accessible VATSIM API. We do not collect data beyond what VATSIM makes publicly available.

3. Data Use

Data is used solely to:

  • Display your current VATSIM flight status on Discord
  • Maintain App functionality and troubleshoot issues

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • Data may be visible to others via Discord's presence system (this is the App's intended function)
  • We may disclose data if required by law

5. Data Storage

  • Temporary caching may occur for performance purposes
  • No long-term storage of personal data beyond operational needs
  • You may request data deletion by discontinuing App use

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with VATSIM and Discord. Their respective privacy policies apply to data they control.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures but cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmission.
